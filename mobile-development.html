Mobile Development

ITgenus offers businesses custom mobile app development services. Our specialists create native mobile applications for Apple iOS, including iPads and iPhones, and Android, as well as progressive web apps (PWAs), and React Native apps.

Our mobile apps are designed to be remarkable. With our specialist mobile app developers, our customer apps have the advantages of a native user-interface, as well as user-experience and hardware feature integration.

What we do

ITgenus mobile application development services include:

  • development of both native iOS and Android clients;
  • full mobile app design, allowing for complete user flow and experience;
  • building a truly independent back-end system, or creating APIs to connect a newly-developed mobile app to a website;
  • integration with a variety of third-party services, including, but not limited to Google Maps, payment systems, social networks, and other software through APIs;
  • converting existing iOS apps into Android or hybrid apps;
  • Quality Assurance, testing, bug fixing and continuing custom development of mobile apps.
